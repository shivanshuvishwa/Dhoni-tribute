Cricketer, India

10 Things You Didn't Know About M.S. Dhoni

  1. He's a car freak, and his favourite is a Hummer H2.
  2. Not just a car freak, he's a motorcycle freak as well, owning 23 bikes (as of date). His favourite, reportedly, is his Harley Davidson.
  3. Many of his cars have the license number '007'.
  4. His hair cut was modelled on the hairstyle of his 'her', John Abraham.
  5. Dhoni was a football goalkeeper. His football coach sent him to play cricket for a local club team, Commando Cricket Club.
  6. Dhoni's father and his wife Sakshi's father both worked for the same company, MECON.
  7. Dhoni was the most expensive signing of the first IPL auction, contracted by the Chennai Super Kings for $1.5 million.
  8. On his ODI debut, Dhoni scored a duck against Bangladesh, out run out.
  9. Dhoni received a two-match ban from playing in the ODI series against Sri Lanka for the team's slow over-rate during the second ODI in Nagpur (18 December 2009) by ICC match referee Jeff Crowe.
  10. Dhoni has the highest average among Indian batsmen who have played more than 50 matches.
